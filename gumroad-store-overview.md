## Niche Filter Criteria

- **Demand:** Real search traffic / existing sales on similar products
- **Competition:** Under 20 strong competitors in the space
- **Margin:** High perceived value, low production cost (digital)
- **Create-ability:** Something I can build via automation/scripts or GotMinerals can execute
- **Price point:** £5-£50 (sweet spot for Gumroad impulse buys)
